# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

EduAI exists to help Indian students access education support they deserve. This community is built around that shared purpose.

We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming, respectful experience for everyone — regardless of background, education level, caste, religion, gender, or technical experience.

## Our Standards

**Behaviour we encourage:**
- Constructive, specific feedback on code and documentation
- Welcoming questions from people new to open source or to government schemes
- Giving credit where it's due
- Being patient — contributors are volunteers

**Behaviour we don't tolerate:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissive responses to genuine questions
- Using this space to promote political views unrelated to education access

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. The project team reserves the right to remove contributions or ban participants who violate this code.
