NEW SCHEME
PM-YASASVI Scholarship 2025 Applications Begin
YASASVI Pre-Matric and Post-Matric scholarships for OBC, EBC, and DNT students — up to ₹1.25 lakh/year. Exam-based selection.
📅 Oct 15, 2025 · Ministry of Social Justice

ANNOUNCEMENT
CUET UG 2026 Registration Opens January 2026
National Testing Agency (NTA) will open CUET UG 2026 applications from January 15. Over 260+ central universities participate.
📅 Oct 18, 2025 · NTA / UGC
💰
POLICY UPDATE
UGC Increases NET JRF Monthly Stipend
UGC has revised JRF fellowship from ₹31,000 to ₹37,000/month effective from this year. SRF stipend increased to ₹42,000/month.
📅 Oct 10, 2025 · UGC
🔬
RESEARCH
ANRF Opens First Funding Call for Young Researchers
Anusandhan National Research Foundation (ANRF) invites research grant applications from early-career researchers. Grants up to ₹50 lakh.
📅 Oct 5, 2025 · ANRF / DST
🏫
ADMISSION
JEE Main 2026 Exam Dates Announced
JEE Main 2026 Session 1 scheduled for January 22–30, 2026. Session 2 in April. Registrations begin December 2025 on jeemain.nta.nic.in.
